HOSTEL FACILITES

We understand that accommodation is an important part when you are looking for a college to join. Hence the college has a full-fledged hostel facility for boys & girls separately inside the campus. 

The College has provided separate hostel blocks for boys and girls with full time resident Hostel wardens for both. The Hostels have excellent facilities such as well ventilated, spacious rooms with high quality furniture, separate study table and cupboard for each resident and attached bathrooms.

There is a hygienic mess serving wholesome meals throughout the day in a separate Dining Hall. The recreation room has facilities for indoor games, magazines & periodicals. A designated area has been provided for outdoor games also. There is 24 hours running water with heaters and the electric supply is uninterrupted with the help of power generating sets.

The hostels can accommodate up-to 400 boys and around 250 girls. Hostel rooms are provided within college campus and are coupled with all facilities that include:

Furnished Rooms

24 X 7 Power Backup

AC & Cooler Rooms

Wi-fi

Nutritious Food

Laundry

24 X 7 Security

First Aid Facility

RO Water

Gym Access
